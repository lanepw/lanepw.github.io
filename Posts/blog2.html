Here: a virtual tour of the University of Sydney

This site is a virtual tour of the University of Sydney. Many colleges have sites like this, but Sydney's is a good example- it features a 360 degree view, controllable by the viewers, at all times. The viewer can also pause the tour at any time to reveal more information and links. The film itself moves from view to view, showing either a time-lapse of activity on campus or a real-time video of students studying, hanging out, etc.
